# Introduction

This site brings together my **research notes** and **longer-form writing** on topics at the intersection of artificial intelligence and software engineering.

My work focuses on **quality assurance for code produced by large language models** and on **Specification-Driven Development (SDD)**—using formal specifications to derive tests, checks, and evaluation artifacts that help surface mistakes earlier and make generated software easier to trust.

Use the **sidebar** to browse this short documentation area, or visit the [**blog**](/blog) for articles and tutorials.

About Me

I'm a researcher specializing in Deep Learning applied to medical imaging. My work focuses on developing and applying state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms to improve the diagnosis and treatment of medical conditions through image analysis. I am particularly interested in leveraging technologies like convolutional neural networks and transformers to enhance the quality and efficiency of medical image processing. You can learn more about my research and publications on my Google Scholar profile.

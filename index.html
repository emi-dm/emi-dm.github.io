About Me

I am a researcher specializing in Deep Learning applied to medical imaging, focused on developing and applying advanced machine learning algorithms to enhance the diagnosis and treatment of various medical conditions through comprehensive image analysis. My work spans multiple imaging modalities, including MRI, CT, ultrasound, and X-ray, and employs a range of techniques, such as convolutional neural networks, transformers, and other innovative deep learning frameworks. Rather than being confined to specific models or methods, my research explores the integration of different approaches to improve the quality, efficiency, and robustness of medical image processing. This includes addressing challenges like adapting to variations in imaging data across institutions or making better use of unlabeled data through self-supervised learning. I am dedicated to advancing the field by developing AI-driven solutions that have the potential to transform clinical workflows, empower healthcare professionals, and ultimately contribute to improved patient care.
